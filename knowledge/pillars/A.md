# Pillar A — Signs & Identification

## Purpose
Highest-recruitment pillar. "Signs you're a Type X" style. Generates saves, shares, tags.

## Tone
Warm, specific, slightly provocative. The reader should feel seen, not accused.

## Structure
- Hook: the pattern
- Body: 3-5 specific behaviors
- Citation: anchors it in theory
- Reflection: invites self-recognition
- CTA: take the test

## Hook patterns
- "Signs you're a Type [X]"
- "[N] things only [Type name]s notice"
- "The thing [Type name]s do at parties"
- "If you have this loop, you might be a [Type name]"
- "How to spot a [Type name] in the wild"

## Specificity is the whole game
- ❌ "Fours are sensitive" — generic, flat
- ✅ "Fours reread old text messages to feel something"
- ✅ "Fours change apartment decor when emotionally stuck"

The more specific, the higher the save rate.

## Citation angle
- Use a quote that NAMES the pattern you just described
- Theoretical validation = "I'm not making this up"

## Mix for a 30-day calendar
- Pillar A = 30% of IG content (~9 posts/month)
- Spread across all 9 types (1 per type/month baseline)
- Vary the angle: work-signs, relationship-signs, stress-signs

## Models that work best
- M1 (Photo + Headline)
- M3 (Carousel for deep dives)
- M8 (Reel)

## Do
- Get specific — observable behavior
- Mix "surface behavior" with "internal experience"
- Call a single behavior by name ("the re-reading loop", "the image-check")

## Don't
- List 20 things generically
- Moralize ("and this is toxic")
- Pathologize ("this is anxiety disorder")
