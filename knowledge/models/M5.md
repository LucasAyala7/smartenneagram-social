# M5 — Citation Card (DNA of the profile)

## When to use
- Pillar B (Citation of the Day) — daily series
- Any time the citation itself is the star
- IG Feed + LinkedIn (clean, shareable)
- Trigger: identification, validation

## Why this is the most important model
This is the ONLY model that 100% leverages our 5,513-citation advantage visually. Competitors cannot replicate it without doing the work. Run a daily series of M5 for 60-90 days and you establish the brand as "the cited one."

## Format
- Square (1080x1080) or portrait (1080x1350)
- Textured paper background
- Large elegant serif quote
- Thin divider
- Author + work + year + page in caps, small, letter-spaced
- Tiny brand mark bottom

## Typography discipline
- **Quote**: serif (Playfair, EB Garamond, Cormorant), 48-60pt, line-height 1.25
- **Divider**: 1px or 2px line, 60px wide, centered
- **Attribution**: sans-serif (Inter), 12pt, CAPS, letter-spacing 2px
- **Brand mark**: Inter 10pt, opacity 60%

## Image prompt guidance
- **NO people** in M5
- Prompt is for the BACKGROUND only
- Parchment / cream / off-white paper texture
- Subtle staining, age spots, fibrous edges
- Optional: coffee ring, dust specks, folded edge
- Palette: warm whites (#F5F0E6), deep charcoal text (#1A1A1A)
- Reference: Penguin Classics covers, New Directions editions

## Copy-side strategy
- Hook = a paraphrase of the quote in modern English
- Insight = unpack what the author meant
- Citation block in caption ≠ the one in the image (or you can repeat — both work)
- Reflection = how this lands for the reader
- CTA = "This is 1 of 5,513 citations our test uses. Find yours — link in bio."

## Series-ability
- Build 30 M5 cards in advance as a "Citation of the Day" content bank
- Rotate across all 9 types and 9 authors
- Prioritize citations WITH page number (44% of acervo)
- Anti-repetition: never repost same citation within 60 days

## Common mistakes
- ❌ Cluttered backgrounds (the quote is the art)
- ❌ Centered attribution in title-case (use caps + letter-spacing)
- ❌ Long quotes (80 words max on card; longer ones go to carousel M3)
- ❌ Inventing a page number — if page = 0, render without page
