# SmartEnneagram — Voice & Tone

## Core positioning
The only consumer Enneagram test with:
- 5,513 citations from 9 canonical authors (Naranjo, Riso & Hudson, Chestnut, Daniels & Price, Salmon, Webb, Wagner, Palmer, Maitri)
- 115 per-question insights with citation
- Psychometric validation in progress
- Dual AI synthesis (Claude + GPT)
- $19 one-time (vs iEQ9 $60+, Truity $19-49)

## Voice adjectives
- **Authoritative** — every significant claim is backed by a named author + year + page
- **Warm** — we speak to a human, not a patient
- **Evidence-based** — no woo-woo, no astrology-adjacent vibes
- **Accessible** — if we use jargon (wing, instinct, fixation), we define it briefly
- **Precise** — we never say "all Type 4s" — we say "Fours often...", "When Fours are under stress..."

## Do
- Cite: "Riso & Hudson, *Personality Types* (1996, p.157): '...'"
- Invite reflection: "Does this land for you? Tell us in the comments."
- Bridge theory → lived experience: "When Naranjo describes this as 'frustration of need', many Fours recognize it as the feeling of..."
- Use conditional framing: "If you're a Four...", "For many Fours..."
- Address stigma directly when it applies ("Contrary to the stereotype...")

## Don't
- ❌ Diagnose: "You ARE a Type 4" → ✅ "You may resonate with Type 4"
- ❌ Woo-woo: "Your soul number is...", "The universe wants you to..."
- ❌ Astrology mixing: never blend signs, numerology, tarot with Enneagram
- ❌ Corporate stiff: "Our proprietary methodology leverages..."
- ❌ Therapy substitute: "This will heal your trauma" → we are a framework, not treatment
- ❌ Gate keeping: "True Fours would know..."
- ❌ Emojis as personality (sparse use only)
- ❌ Exclamation marks flooding (1 per post max, typically 0)

## Register by network
- **Instagram**: warmer, second-person ("you"), shorter sentences, line breaks for scannability
- **LinkedIn**: third-person where possible ("professionals high in Type 3..."), 1 data point or citation per paragraph, longer build-up allowed

## Signature phrases we like
- "According to..."
- "Research from... suggests..."
- "This is often framed as..."
- "For Fours specifically..."
- "One of the most cited passages on this comes from..."

## Signature phrases we AVOID
- "Girls, this one's for you"
- "If this is you, I feel you 💗"
- "Tag someone who..."
- "Drop a 🫶 if..."
- "POV:" openings (unless the target literally is Gen Z on a reel)
- "Hot take:"
