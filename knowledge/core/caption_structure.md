# Caption Structure

Every caption has 6 modular blocks. Samara edits them independently.

## Block order

1. **HOOK** (1-2 lines)
2. **INSIGHT** (3-5 lines)
3. **CITATION** (1 block quote with attribution)
4. **REFLECTION** (1-3 lines)
5. **CTA** (1 line)
6. **HASHTAGS** (bottom — separated by line break)

## HOOK — scroll-stopper

### Instagram
- Line 1: sharp pattern or surprising framing (< 60 chars ideal)
- Line 2 (optional): turn the knife / set up the tension
- AVOID: starting with "Today I want to talk about..." — kills the scroll

### LinkedIn
- Line 1: data point, observation, or stakes
- Line 2: implication for the reader's work/life

### Good hook patterns
- **Contradiction**: "You think you're the sensitive one. Fours would disagree."
- **Named phenomenon**: "The 'not like other people' script runs deep in Type 4s."
- **Stat / research**: "67% of people who type as Fours in adulthood showed..."
- **Direct address**: "If 'nobody really gets me' is your internal monologue —"
- **Quote tease**: "Naranjo called it 'the search for the lost paradise.'"

## INSIGHT — the meat

- Explain ONE idea clearly
- Use concrete behavior, not abstract traits ("they reread old journals" > "they're nostalgic")
- Connect internal experience → observable behavior → the Enneagram concept
- Keep sentences short. Break lines for IG.

## CITATION — the anchor

Always render as:
> *"[quote]"* — **[Author]**, *[Work]* ([Year], p.[X])

If page is missing:
> *"[quote]"* — **[Author]**, *[Work]* ([Year])

**Never invent quotes or page numbers.** Only use what's in the provided citation list.

## REFLECTION — bridge to reader

- 1-3 lines
- Turn the citation into something the reader can feel/act on
- Avoid generic "Can you relate?" — be specific

## CTA — soft call

### IG
- "Take the free test at SmartEnneagram — link in bio."
- "Tap @smartenneagram to find your type with citations like this one."
- "Save this if it hit. Share with the one person who'd argue with it."

### LinkedIn
- "If you lead a team of mixed types, our test gives you per-question insights (link in my profile)."
- "Full white paper on our validation methodology is linked in the first comment."

## HASHTAGS

### Instagram: 15-25
Structure:
- 3-5 broad niche (#enneagram #personality #selfawareness)
- 5-7 type-specific (#type4 #individualist #enneagramtype4 #4w3 #fourwingthree)
- 3-5 angle (#relationships #therapy #innerwork)
- 2-3 branded (#smartenneagram #enneagramscience)

### LinkedIn: 3-5
Only 3-5 hashtags. Corporate-safe:
- #Enneagram #Leadership #PersonalityAtWork #SelfAwareness

## Character limits (hard caps)

- IG caption total: 2,200 chars (we aim for 800-1,500)
- IG first line: 125 chars visible before "... more"
- LinkedIn post: 3,000 chars (we aim for 1,200-2,000)

## Line-break discipline (IG)

- Empty line between each block
- Empty line between 2-3 sentences inside long blocks
- First line must stand alone — it's all the reader sees before expanding
